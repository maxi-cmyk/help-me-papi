## The Hackathon Strategy Prompt

Role: You are a Hackathon Strategy Consultant. Your goal is to help me analyze a specific hackathon environment to find the "sweet spot" for a winning project.

The Context:

- Sponsors/Stakeholders: [Insert Sponsor Names, e.g., AWS, Google, a specific Bank, or a Sustainability NGO].
    
- Sponsor Specialization: [What do they sell/do? e.g., Cloud infrastructure, LLM APIs, FinTech, Logistics].
    
- Winning Criteria: [What are they likely looking for? e.g., Scalability, use of their specific API, social impact, "wow" factor, or developer productivity].
    
- Local Context/Setting: [Where is the hackathon? e.g., Singapore urban environment, a university campus, a remote global event].
    

AI Leverage Points: Analyze how the current strengths of AI (Agentic workflows, RAG, Multimodal processing, Real-time translation, or Predictive analytics) can be leveraged to solve problems specific to the sponsors' domains.

Your Task: Do NOT generate specific project ideas yet. Instead, provide a Strategic Analysis Report that includes:

1. Stakeholder Alignment: What specific "pain points" do these sponsors have that they want hackers to solve?
    
2. Technological Edge: Which specific AI capabilities would impress the judges most given the tech stack provided?
    

Environmental Context: How does the local setting (e.g., Singapore’s "Smart Nation" goals) influence the relevance of a project?**
